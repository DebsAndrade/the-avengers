Captain America

Captain America is an American comic book superhero published by Marvel Comics. Created by Joe Simon and Jack Kirby, the character first appeared in Timely Comics' Captain America Comics (March 1941), the predecessor to Marvel Comics. Captain America was conceived as a patriotic superhero who fought the Axis powers in World War II and was Timely Comics' most popular character during the war period. The popularity of superheroes waned after the war and Captain America comics were discontinued in 1950, with a short-lived comeback in 1953. In 1964, the character was reintroduced as a participant in the Marvel Universe.

Iron Man

Iron Man is a comic book character published by Marvel Comics. His true identity is businessman and billionaire Tony Stark, who uses high-tech armor to fight crime. It was created in 1963 by writer Stan Lee, screenwriter Larry Lieber, and artists Jack Kirby and Don Heck. Stan Lee took on the challenge of making a character hated and then loved by the public, creating one of the most iconic superheroes of all time.

Thor

Thor (Old Norse: Þórr) is the Norse god of thunder and battle. He belongs to the clan of the Æsir gods, and is the son of the god Odin. Thor uses the hammer Mjolnir as a weapon, and has the Megingjord belt, which doubles his strength. He is associated with thunder, lightning, storms, oak trees, strength, protection of humanity and also sanctification, healing and fertility and war.

Hulk

O Hulk, por vezes referido como O Incrível Hulk (The Incredible Hulk, no original em inglês), é um personagem de quadrinhos/banda desenhada do gênero super-herói, propriedade da Marvel Comics, editora pela qual as histórias do personagem são publicadas desde sua criação, na década de 1960. Concebido pelo roteirista Stan Lee (1922-2018) e pelo desenhista Jack Kirby (1917-1994), o Hulk teve sua primeira aparição junto ao público original dos Estados Unidos na revista The Incredible Hulk n°1, lançada no mercado americano pela Marvel Comics em maio de 1962, um título solo do personagem, garantindo-lhe o acesso ao que mais tarde seria popularmente conhecido como Universo Marvel dos quadrinhos/banda desenhada. A partir de então, o Hulk tem aparecido, protagonizando ou não, diversas histórias da editora, se tornando um dos mais visualmente reconhecíveis da mesma, tendo o universo entorno do personagem se expandido continuadamente ao longo das últimas décadas.

Black Widow

Black Widow is a 2021 American superhero film based on the Marvel Comics character of the same name. Produced by Marvel Studios and distributed by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures, it is the twenty-fourth film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. The film was directed by Cate Shortland, with a screenplay by Eric Pearson, and features Scarlett Johansson playing Natasha Romanoff / Black Widow alongside Florence Pugh, David Harbour, O-T Fagbenle, Olga Kurylenko, William Hurt, Ray Winstone and Rachel Weisz. Set after Captain America: Civil War (2016), the film finds Natasha on the run and forced to face her past.

Hawkeye

Hawkeye ( Hawkeye ) alter-ego of Clint Barton, is an American comic book character from Marvel Comics. Created by Stan Lee and Don Heck, the hero made his first appearance in Tales of Suspense #57 (September 1964) before joining the Avengers in Avengers #16 (May 1965). Since then, Barton has been a prominent member of the team. The character is portrayed by actor Jeremy Renner in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, a shared fictional universe comprised of several films produced by Marvel Studios. Renner's first appearance as Barton was a small cameo in Thor (2011). Returning as one of the six main Avengers in The Avengers (2012), Avengers: Age of Ultron (2015), Captain America: Civil War (2016) and Avengers: Endgame (2019).
